About us

Arceo Group is a Nordic liquidity house. We help investors and owner-led companies realise value from locked or distressed assets – lawfully, discreetly, fast.

How we work: assess the situation, analyse options, and implement a contingency plan – including correct loss‑deduction documentation (förlustavdrag) where applicable.

Our purpose: free real value when capital is stuck and set a clear liquidity standard in the Nordics.
